About Me

Roughly two years ago, I decided it was time to redefine myself professionally. Two years departed from the financial services industry and now working for a SaaS company, I was learning how deeply-entrenched all industries are in web-based engagement solutions and cloud-computing. It didn't take me long to understand that growth was necessary to work in this industry and prepare to pursue advancement opportunities. This journey began with a conversaton with a coworker who is a front-end developer.

Born and raised a Hoosier, a native of Gary and an Indiana University alum, I bring years of account management, sales, and entrepreneurial experience to merge with the skill I hope to obtain as a web developer.
